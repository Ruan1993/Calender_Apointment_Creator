Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 28, 2025

Appointment Maker Pro ("the App") helps you create and manage appointments. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address when you sign up or sign in.
  • Appointment details including client name, date, time, services, notes, and contact phone.
  • Contacts selected via your device (if you choose to pick contacts).
  • Basic usage and analytics events to improve the App.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core features such as creating, storing, and viewing appointments.
  • Send optional reminders and WhatsApp messages if you choose to use those features.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience.

Data Storage and Sharing

  • Appointment data is stored in your Firebase project under your user ID.
  • We do not sell personal information.
  • Data may be processed by service providers (e.g., Firebase) to operate the App.

Retention

Data is retained as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You may delete appointments or contacts at any time.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures, including Firebase security rules, to protect data. No method is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • Access, update, or delete your data within the App.
  • Opt out of reminders by disabling them on appointments.
  • Sign out at any time from the gear menu.

Children

The App is not directed to children under 13. Do not use the App if you are under 13.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. The updated version will be posted here with a new effective date.
